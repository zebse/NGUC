New Generation University College

New Generation University College (NGUC) was founded in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia in 2002. The founders, Matthew Gichille and Spouse, aimed to provide superior education and total quality higher education opportunities to the younger generation of Ethiopia and beyond. They had taken into account the great demand and needs of the country for quality private higher education to meet the increased demand for trained manpower.

Core values

Core Values are the fundamental values or ideals at the heart of the organization. The core values of NGUC are:

  • Effective management delivers development benefits to Ethiopia.
  • New technologies and knowledge overcome development barriers.
  • Shared knowledge solves community problems together.
  • Continuous learning and Innovativeness.
  • Effective Community Based Quality Service.
  • Maximum utilization of capacity and resources.
